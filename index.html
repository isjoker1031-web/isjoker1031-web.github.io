Information We Collect

Our apps do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. All game data (scores, progress, settings) is stored locally on your device only.

Advertising

Our apps display advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA/IDFV) — only with your explicit consent
  • IP address and general location
  • Device type, OS version, and app usage data

We request your permission before using your IDFA for ad personalization, in compliance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. You may opt out at any time in your device's Privacy settings.

For more information on how Google uses data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

App Tracking Transparency

In accordance with Apple's ATT policy, our apps will ask for your permission before tracking your activity for advertising purposes. You can change this permission at any time in Settings → Privacy → Tracking.

Data Retention

We do not retain any user data on our servers. Any data collected by AdMob is governed by Google's own privacy policy and data retention practices.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are rated 4+ and are suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The advertising displayed is subject to Google's child-directed treatment settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
